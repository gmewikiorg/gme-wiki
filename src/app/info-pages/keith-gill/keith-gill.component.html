Keith Gill
Keith Gill, AKA Roaring Kitty, AKA DeepFuckingValue
Summary
  • Roaring Kitty (or RK), also known as DeepFuckingValue (or DFV), real name Keith Gill, is an individual that confidently believes in his GME investment thesis
  • Starting with an approximately $50,000 investment in GME stock and GME call options in 2019, Roaring Kitty was able to increase the value of his position to several hundred million dollars as of June 2024
Timeline
  • Roaring Kitty began to gain popularity on social media in 2020 for his contrarian but confident GME position
  • As investors joined in and bought GME, eventually the share price of GME started to rise dramatically, which drew more attention, which caused more buying, until the eventual January 2021 sneeze
    • For this, Keith Gill was questioned by the U.S. House Committee on Financial Services for his involvement
    • The movie Dumb Money (2023) tells the story of that series of events
  • Roaring Kitty / DeepFuckingValue disappeared from social media in 2021
  • Roaring Kitty / DeepFuckingValue returned 3 years later in 2024 showing that not only did he continue to hold his GME but in fact was able to increase the size of his position substantially to over several hundred million dollars
