Outcome of GameStop's completion of 3 at-the-market equity offering programs
In 2024, GameStop raised nearly $3.5 billion in cash from 3 ATMs in exchange for shares
Summary
  • On 3 occassions in 2024, GameStop has raised cash by selling shares
  • On May 24, 2024, GameStop completed the first of 3 ATMs , raising $0.933 billion
  • On June 11, 2024, GameStop completed the second of 3 ATMs , raising an additional $2.137 billion
  • "Having a strong balance sheet especially in times of economic uncertainty is a strategic advantage."
    -- Ryan Cohen, GameStop 2024 Annual Meeting (June 17)
  • On September 23, 2024, GameStop completed the third of 3 ATMs , raising an additional $0.4 billion
