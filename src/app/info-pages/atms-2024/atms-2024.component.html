Outcome of GameStop's completion of 2 at-the-market equity offering programs
GameStop raised more than $3 billion in cash from 2 ATMs in exchange for shares
Summary
  • GameStop has taken advantage of the rising market price of GME shares by issuing and selling shares for cash
  • On May 24, GameStop completed the first of 2 ATMs , raising $0.933 billion
  • On June 11, GameStop completed the second of 2 ATMs , raising an additional $2.137 billion
  • "Having a strong balance sheet especially in times of economic uncertainty is a strategic advantage."
    -- Ryan Cohen, GameStop 2024 Annual Meeting (June 17)
