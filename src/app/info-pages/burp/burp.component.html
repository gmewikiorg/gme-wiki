May 2024 GME Burp

Why did the price of GME soar in May 2024?
Summary
  • Following the strong earnings report that showed full-year profitability for the first time in 6 years, the share price of GME suddenly soared in May 2024
  • This event coincided with the return of Roaring Kitty
  • Some may observe that The Burp occurred approximately 35 trading days after GameStop released the FY23 earnings report
  • GameStop capitalized on the high-volume
