Household Investors, aka Retail Investors

A prominent faction of the GME saga
Summary
  • Household GME shareholders have played an important role in the GME saga since approximately January 2021
  • Hundreds of thousands of individuals, communicating over social media, learn and share information pertaining to GME and the market
  • This faction has directly registered millions of shares of GME, representing the single largest specific portion of GME ownership
  • The number of registered shares of GME has stagnated since 2022 but remains steady, with about 90% of all registered shares staying in place since direct registering began in 2021
  • The board of directors and this faction of GME shareholders have an alignment of interests. For example, the CEO Ryan Cohen receives no compensation and only holds shares, ensuring appropriate incentive to improve the value of the stock for all shareholders
  • The board of directors retains strong support from retail investors, as demonstrated by voting outcomes in each annual shareholder meeting since 2021
